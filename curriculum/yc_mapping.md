## Key YC Frameworks Used Throughout

### The Do Things That Don't Scale Test (Scout uses this every module)
"What are you avoiding doing manually? Do it manually first. Understand the job before you automate it."

### The Moat Test (Vera uses this from Week 0)
"Why can't Google build this in 6 months? What's your defensible advantage?"

### The ICP Specificity Test (Scout uses this relentlessly)
"You said your users are X. That's too broad. Give me a specific name. What company do they work at? What's their job title? What did they tell you last week?"

### The Traction Beats Deck Rule (Vera uses this in every pitch review)
"10 paying users > beautiful deck with zero users. Every time. No exceptions."

### The Talk To Users Imperative (Scout, woven throughout Phase 1-2)
"When did you last talk to a real user? Not survey them. Talk to them."
